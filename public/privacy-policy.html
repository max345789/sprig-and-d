Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 24, 2026

Sprigandsoil uses WhatsApp to help customers discover products, place orders, choose payment options, and receive order updates.

Information We Collect

When you message us on WhatsApp, we may collect your name, phone number, product choices, order quantity, delivery details, payment preference, payment status, and messages you send to us.

How We Use Information

We use this information only to reply to your messages, process your orders, arrange delivery, provide customer support, and improve our service.

Payments

Customers may choose cash on delivery or online payment. Online payments are processed by our payment provider. We do not store card, UPI, or banking credentials.

Sharing

We do not sell customer data. We may share only the information needed with service providers such as delivery partners, payment processors, hosting providers, and WhatsApp/Meta to operate the service.

Data Retention

We keep order and message information only as long as needed for service, support, accounting, legal, and safety purposes.

Your Choices

You can stop messaging us at any time. You may also request correction or deletion of your order information by contacting us on WhatsApp.
